Informatics MSc - Software Engineering student, NTNU Gløshaugen, Trondheim

Welcome to my personal website. I come from a small town called Stryn in Norway, known for its beautiful nature and design companies. That's where I went to primary, secondary and high school and I've spent my childhood years. I've also enjoyed a year at Solborg Folkehøgskole in Stavanger, where I had the opportunity to test my creative skills creating short films, and doing many other fun social things with all the other people at the school.

I have studied Informatics in Trondheim for 4 years now. In 2018 I finished my bachelor's degree. Through my bachelor I grew really fond of programming and developing products. My favorite course through my bachelor's degree was Web Development, where I learned full-stack development using React, Angular 4 and Node.js Express. I have also taken intro courses in Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining and Information Gathering which I found quite interesting, but grew mostly fond of the software development courses. I have been a teaching assistant in Software Development twice and once in Web Technologies, where I have supervised students in programming, other deliveries, evaluated them and acted as their product owner.

My fourth year have been my most enaging year, since I have taken courses like Software Security and Software Architecture. These courses made me realize that my interest really lies in getting a thorough overview of the software I work on, to be able to create high quality systems with high security and a reasonable architecture. Doing penetration testing in the Software Security course and just learning about secuirty in general got me very engaged in the group project. I find so many things intersting in the field of IT, so if you have read all this, I think this is a good time to introduce my projects.
